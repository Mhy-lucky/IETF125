# My IETF New Participant Experience

## 1、Question about Rough Consensus
I have learned IETF's famous proverb is："We reject: kings, presidents and voting. We believe in: rough consensus and running code." 理解rough consensus对有效参与IETF至关重要。

During Session 3 (Standards Development), I learned from Barry Leiba about IETF's "rough consensus" process. This raised questions about how to determine when consensus is reached and how to handle persistent objections.
### My Question：
- 既然不是投票，怎么判断"rough consensus"已经达成了
- 如果有人一直反对，如何决定是继续讨论还是可以推进
- "rough consensus"到底在实践中如何运作
---
Then  I went to study the relevant standards,and here are my thoughts and summary.

## 📄 relevant RFC

**RFC 7282 - "On Consensus and Humming in the IETF"**

**https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc7282.html**

### 📚 RFC 7282's core content

| section | title | Corresponding to my question |
|------|------|--------------|
| Section 2 | "Lack of disagreement is more important than agreement" | 怎么判断consensus？ |
| Section 3 | "Rough consensus is achieved when all issues are addressed, but not necessarily accommodated" | 有人反对怎么办？ |
| Section 6 | "One hundred people for and five people against might not be rough consensus" | 人数多不一定赢 |
| Section 7 | "Five people for and one hundred people against might still be rough consensus" | 少数人的技术理由可能成立 |

### 💡 关键引述

RFC 7282中对rough consensus的定义：

> **"Consensus is when everyone is sufficiently satisfied with the chosen solution, such that they no longer have specific objections to it."**

关于如何处理反对意见：

> **"If the chair of a working group determines that a technical issue brought forward by an objector has been truly considered by the working group, and the working group has made an informed decision that the objection has been answered or is not enough of a technical problem to prevent moving forward, the chair can declare that there is rough consensus to go forward, the objection notwithstanding."**
>
> （如果工作组主席确定反对者提出的技术问题已经被工作组真正考虑过，并且工作组已经做出知情决定——即反对意见已被解答或不足以构成技术问题阻碍推进——那么主席可以宣布有rough consensus继续推进，尽管有反对意见。）

### 🌟 Keywords

**Technical objection must be addressed; non-technical objection can be disregarded.**
